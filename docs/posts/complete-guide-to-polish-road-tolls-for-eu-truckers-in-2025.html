Complete Guide to Polish Road Tolls for EU Truckers in 2025

For EU-based trucking companies operating in Poland, understanding road toll requirements is essential for smooth, cost-effective logistics. With tighter EU regulations, evolving national policies, and the need to avoid costly fines or delays, 2025 brings both continuity and potential shifts in how Polish infrastructure charges are applied to heavy goods vehicles (HGVs). This comprehensive guide covers everything fleet managers need to know: the current toll system, compliance requirements, cost-saving strategies, and how Poland’s charges fit into the broader European transport landscape.

The Polish Road Toll System in 2025: An Overview

Poland maintains a distance-based toll system for trucks registered outside the EU (including many EU members under specific transitional rules) and time/distance-based charges for Polish-registered vehicles. For foreign EU truckers, the key points for 2025 are:
